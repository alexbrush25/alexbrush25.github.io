The Salt Lake Temple

The fourth temple constructed by the LDS Church. Since its dedication on 6 April 1893, the Salt Lake temple has been a beacon to the members of The Church of Jesus Christ of Latter Day Saints. The temple's six gothic spires tower high above the Salt Lake skyline. A beautiful sight to see. Temple ordanances are almost always booked far in advance.
